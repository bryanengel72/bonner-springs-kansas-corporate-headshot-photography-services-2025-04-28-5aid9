Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical headshot session take?

Our standard sessions typically run 60-90 minutes, including outfit changes and different poses. This allows ample time for warming up, professional guidance, and capturing a variety of expressions and angles.

What should I wear to my headshot session?

We recommend bringing 2-3 professional outfits in solid colors that complement your skin tone. Avoid busy patterns or trendy styles that may date quickly. We'll provide detailed guidance when you book your session.

How quickly will I receive my photos?

You'll receive a gallery of proofs within 48 hours of your session. After selecting your favorites, final retouched images are delivered within 5 business days. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

Do you offer on-location services for companies?

Yes! We provide on-site corporate headshot services for teams of 5 or more. We'll bring our professional lighting and backdrop setup to your location for a convenient and consistent experience.

What about retouching and editing?

All final images include professional retouching to ensure you look your best while maintaining a natural appearance. This includes skin smoothing, removal of temporary blemishes, and color correction.
